Language

Estonian Language:
Finnic family, closely related to Finnish
Unique Baltic letters: Ä, Ö, Ü, Õ

Example sentence:

"Ühel suvehommikul käisin pargis ja nägin oravat mustikaid söömas. Õhk lõhnas värskelt ja päikesepaiste soojendas mu nägu. Mõtlesin, et soome loodus on ilus igal aastaajal."

("On a summer morning, I walked in the park and saw a squirrel eating blueberries. The air smelled fresh, and the sunshine warmed my face. I thought that Finnish nature is beautiful in all seasons.")

Landscape & Infrastructure

Estonian Architecture:

  • Materials: Wood + white brick buildings
  • Roofs: Steep corrugated metal
  • Walls: Painted stone walls (somewhat common)
  • More wooden structures than in Latvia/Lithuania


Estonian Bollards:

  • Baton-shaped and round design
  • Front: 1 rectangular reflector (white/orange)
  • Back: 2 circular reflectors (white/orange)

Estonian Utility Poles:
Wooden poles with small metal caps (like Nordic designs)

Baltic Kilometer Markers:
Same blue design in all Baltic countries
Estonia:
Perpendicular to road, numbers on both sides

Road Signs & Lanes

Estonian Pedestrian Signs:

  • 3 white stripes
  • Detailed silhouette (shoes + distinct head shape)

Estonian Road Signs:

  • Thin red border extending to the sign's edge
  • No white outline

Estonian Directional Signs:
Blue background with white text/outline
